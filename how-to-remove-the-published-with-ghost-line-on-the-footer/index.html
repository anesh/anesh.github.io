How to remove the ‘published with ghost’ line on the footer of a Ghost Blog

Anesh Kesavan

Oct 19, 2021 • 1 min read

The below instructions are for the default theme, if you need help with other themes, please subscribe to the blog and send me your question.

Go to the below destination of your self-hosted ghost installation

/var/www/blog/content/themes/casper

And open default.hbs file in your favorite text editor

Go to the below section of code

{{!-- The global footer at the very bottom of the screen --}}
<footer class="site-footer outer">
    <div class="inner">
        <section class="copyright"><a href="{{@site.url}}">{{@site.title}}</a> &copy; {{date format="YYYY"}}</section>
        <nav class="site-footer-nav">
            {{navigation type="secondary"}}
        </nav>
        <div><a href="https://ghost.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Powered by Ghost</a></div>
    </div>
</footer>

And in the above section change line 75 to like below

 <div style="display:none"><a href="https://ghost.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Powered by Ghost</a></div>
{{!-- The global footer at the very bottom of the screen --}}
<footer class="site-footer outer">
    <div class="inner">
        <section class="copyright"><a href="{{@site.url}}">{{@site.title}}</a> &copy; {{date format="YYYY"}}</section>
        <nav class="site-footer-nav">
            {{navigation type="secondary"}}
        </nav>
        <div style="display:none"><a href="https://ghost.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Powered by Ghost</a></div>
    </div>
</footer>
